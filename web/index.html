three keys, that's the whole app

Copy. Summon. Paste.

1

Copy like normal

Text, links, code, images, colours, files, even map locations — Prosciutto captures every copy automatically and quietly.

2

Summon the gallery

Hit ⌘⇧V. A strip of colour-coded cards slides up from the bottom, newest first, without leaving your app.

3

Paste in a keystroke

Arrow to the card, press ⏎. Or ⌘1–9 for pinned favourites. Gone before you blink.
